At One Love Radio, we believe in the unifying power of music. Founded on principles of unity, positivity, and cultural celebration, our station serves as a bridge between communities through the universal language of music.

We're more than just a radio station - we're a movement dedicated to:

  • Promoting African music and artists
  • Creating a platform for emerging talent
  • Fostering community connections
  • Delivering quality entertainment with positive vibes

Build Community

Create spaces (both physical and digital) where music lovers can connect and grow together.

Preserve Culture

Celebrate and preserve African musical heritage while embracing contemporary sounds.
